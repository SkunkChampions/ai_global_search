This data describes a record of expenses and income over 2022 to 2023 for a coffee company. There are numerous categories including revenue, expense, and cost. The type of expense or income falls into to natural buckets, cash in and cash out. The actual amount is also included in US dollars. A detailed description of the source or sink of the funds is found in the description column. This data is available for numerous locations by zipcode and store name, for example Blue Pot 1. This data set can bu used to understand the accounting for this coffee business. Some expenses may not always be necessary and it will be useful to identify which ones are driving our loss in revenue.
